why should we save sharks?

why should we save sharks, its not like they help us right? 

Well no, it's not like that. The question we should really ask is how the shark will impact the ecosystem if we exterminate all of them? Bad things would happen. Firstly, a lot of shark viewing places would run out of business. Then, all the sea animals that eat sharks will die also because they have no food (or they would have to change their diet). The animals who were eaten by sharks will overpopulate because there is no one to eat them. Then, those animals that overpopulate will eat up all the animals or plants that are under them. So basically, long story short, everything would deteriorate and die. We need to stop all these sharks dying or we will have a global crisis on our hands. And as we know... global crises = money loss  and so on and so forth. See what I mean? We need to tell the public all about the sharks that are dying and we need to save them!
